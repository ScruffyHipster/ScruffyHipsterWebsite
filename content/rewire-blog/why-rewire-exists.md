Most phone habits do not start with a decision. They start with a reflex.

Rewire is built around that moment. You choose the apps and websites that pull you away, start a focus session, and let iOS Screen Time controls create a pause when you try to open them.

The point is not to shame the choice. The point is to make the choice visible before the loop has already carried you somewhere else.

## friction with a job

Good friction should be specific. Rewire is not trying to turn your phone into a productivity dashboard or tell you what kind of person you are. It gives you a clear interruption at the point where autopilot usually wins.

That pause is small by design. It gives you enough space to decide whether you want to continue, reset, or get back to the thing you meant to do.

## what the app shows

Rewire keeps the feedback simple. It can show patterns around blocks and sessions without pretending to know the whole story behind every choice.

That matters because focus tools should be truthful. A useful app can give you structure without inventing certainty.
