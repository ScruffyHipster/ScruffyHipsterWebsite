Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Smarty Colours values your privacy. The app does not capture, collect, store, or transmit any user data.

Data Processing

All Smarty Colours experiences are processed entirely on your device using Apple’s Foundation Model or an on-device Llama model. No app data ever leaves your device, and no information is shared with any external servers or third parties.

Analytics and Tracking

Smarty Colours does not use analytics, tracking tools, or advertising technologies.

Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. Since no data is collected, it fully complies with child privacy standards, including COPPA and GDPR-K.
