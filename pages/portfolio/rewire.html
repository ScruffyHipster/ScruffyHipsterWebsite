Rewire

Rewire helps you break impulsive phone habits by interrupting distraction at the moment it happens — and redirecting your attention back to what you intended to do.

Designed to interrupt distraction — not track it

Rewire doesn’t rely on motivation or streaks. It uses iOS system controls to create deliberate friction at the point of distraction, helping users regain control of their attention.

Impulse Interruption

When a blocked app is opened, Rewire intercepts the action and forces a conscious pause before continuing.

System-Level Blocking

Built on Apple’s Screen Time APIs, Rewire enforces limits at the OS level — not via fragile timers or reminders.

Always-On Focus Sessions

Run open-ended or time-boxed sessions that continuously protect your focus throughout the day.

Minimal by Design

No feeds, no gamified noise, no emotional manipulation. Rewire stays out of the way and does its job.

Privacy-first by default

Rewire does not track you across apps or collect behavioural data for advertising. All focus sessions and usage data stay on-device.
